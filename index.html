Morris Avenue Cake House and /Dessert/ Order Online

Morris Avenue Cake House and Dessert is the home to magnificent cakes both wedding and birthday cakes. We also offer cupcakes and dessert tables for birthdays, weddings and for any of your events.

Started from a home kitchen now it is a big company that offers exclusive and uniques cakes to our customers. We cater for every class of customers and have a direct personnel to cater for your cake needs.

Every cupcake and cake we make uses the finest available ingredients and is baked fresh to order in small batches by our committed team. Each one is then hand-decorated by that same small gang of cake-obsessed individuals, dedicated to making sure you enjoy the best darn cake experience you've ever had. No short-cuts, no weird additives or preservatives, just incredibly delicious.
